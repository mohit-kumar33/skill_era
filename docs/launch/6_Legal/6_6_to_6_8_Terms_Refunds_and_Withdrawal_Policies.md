# 6.6 Terms & Conditions, 6.7 Refund & 6.8 Withdrawal Policies

**Project Name:** Apex Arena (MVP)  
**Document Owner:** Legal Department  
**Version:** 1.0.0  

---

## 1. Terms & Conditions (T&C) Core Structure
The T&Cs must be explicitly accepted via a mandatory checkbox during the `/register` flow. 

### 1.1 Structural Clauses
- **Eligibility:** Explicitly states the user must be 18+ and residing outside of banned states.
- **Nature of the Game:** The user acknowledges that Chess is a game of skill and they are voluntarily risking capital based on their own strategic abilities.
- **Fair Play:** Any usage of "Automated Engines", "Bots", "Stockfish", or "Secondary Devices" constitutes a material breach of contract. Apex Arena reserves the right to confiscate all funds (Deposit and Winnings) of caught cheaters.
- **Limitation of Liability:** Apex Arena is not liable for network drops by the user's ISP causing a time forfeit.

## 2. Refund Policy
*This policy must be explicitly linked on the Cashfree deposit gateway.*

1. **No Refunds on Lost Matches:** Once an entry fee is deducted and a match initiates, the fee is non-refundable. Losing a game does not entitle a user to a refund.
2. **Platform Faults:** If the server crashes mid-game resulting in a `CANCELED` state, entry fees are credited back to the `DepositBalance` within 10 minutes.
3. **Deposit Failures:** If a user's bank is debited but the platform `DepositBalance` does not reflect it within 2 hours, the User must contact support. If verified via Cashfree, a gateway refund is issued to the source account (TAT: 5-7 business days).

## 3. Withdrawal Policy
*Designed to protect operations from liquidity shocks and money laundering.*

1. **Mandatory KYC:** No withdrawals, regardless of amount, will be processed without a fully verified Aadhaar and PAN.
2. **The 24-Hour Cooldown:** To prevent rapid chip-dumping, the withdrawal button is completely disabled for 24 hours immediately following any successful deposit.
3. **Minimum/Maximum Limits:**
   - Minimum Withdrawal: ₹200.
   - Maximum Withdrawal (MVP): ₹10,000 per 24 hours.
4. **Processing Times (SLA):** All withdrawals are subject to manual Risk Desk review. Users must be warned that processing can take up to 48 business hours, though typical clearance is under 6 hours.
5. **Destination Restrictions:** Withdrawals will strictly only be routed to a bank account bearing the exact legal name verified on the PAN/Aadhaar card. Third-party payouts are strictly banned to ensure AML compliance.
