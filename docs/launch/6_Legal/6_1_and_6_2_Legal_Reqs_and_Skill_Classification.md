## 1. Legal & Compliance Requirements Document
Operating a Real-Money Gaming (RMG) platform in India mandates absolute compliance with federal frameworks and highly fragmented state-level legislations. Ignorance of state law can result in criminal prosecution of the founders under various state Gaming Acts.

### 1.1 Core Legal Mandates
1. **Age Restriction:** Only adults strictly over 18 years of age.
2. **Geo-Restriction:** Absolutely no operations in states where courts or legislatures have banned RMG (even games of skill).
3. **Tax Compliance:** Mandatory 30% TDS deduction on Net Winnings at source (Section 194BA).
4. **GST Compliance:** Payment of 28% GST on the face value of the initial player deposit (to be absorbed or passed on, but definitively paid).

## 2. Skill vs. Gambling Classification Analysis
The entire existence of Apex Arena relies on the legal distinction established by the Supreme Court of India: wagering on a "Game of Skill" is constitutionally protected as a legitimate business under Article 19(1)(g), while waging on a "Game of Chance" is illegal gambling.

### 2.1 The "Preponderance of Skill" Test
Indian courts utilize the "Preponderance of Skill" test to determine legality. A game qualifies if success depends more upon the superior knowledge, training, attention, experience, and adroitness of the player than upon chance.

### 2.2 Why Chess Qualifies (Our Legal Defense Shield)
1. **Zero Luck Element:** Unlike Rummy (dealing random cards) or Ludo (rolling a random dice), Chess contains exactly zero Random Number Generators (RNG).
2. **Deterministic Start:** Both players start with identical pieces in identical positions.
3. **Complete Information:** All previous moves and current board states are perfectly visible to both players. There is no hidden information.
4. **Conclusion:** Chess represents the purest mathematical manifestation of a game of skill. A Grandmaster will defeat a novice 100 out of 100 times. Therefore, offering entry fees and prize pools for Chess is legally unassailable under current Supreme Court jurisprudence.

- The legal team will pivot the defense entirely around the deterministic, zero-RNG nature of our engine code.
